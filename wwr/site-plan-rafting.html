Overview

Purpose

The main goal of this sport is to maneuver and navigate the raft through rapids or turbulent waters, often times taking you over rocks and drops, without capsizing the raft. In the past, traditional rafts were used to transport people and agricultural products.

Audience

Our target audience for the water rafting website is adventure enthusiasts and thrill-seekers who are looking for an exhilarating experience in the great outdoors. They are typically between the ages of 18 and 45, physically active, and enjoy challenging activities. They value experiences over material possessions and are seeking memorable adventures that push their limits and provide a sense of accomplishment.

Scenario Questions:

Question: What age group is most likely to be interested in our water rafting adventures? Answer: Our target audience is typically between the ages of 18 and 45, as they are more likely to seek out adventurous and physically demanding activities.

Question: What interests or hobbies might our target audience have? Answer: Our target audience is likely to enjoy outdoor activities such as hiking, camping, and rock climbing. They may also have an interest in adrenaline-inducing sports like skydiving or bungee jumping.

Question: What motivates our target audience to choose one adventure experience over another? Answer: Our target audience is motivated by the desire for unique and challenging experiences. They seek adventures that push their limits, provide a sense of accomplishment, and create lasting memories. They value experiences that are thrilling, safe, and well-organized.
